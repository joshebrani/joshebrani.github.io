More about me

I graduated from CUNY Queens College with a dual major in Corporate Finance and Economics, working as a financial analyst at a commercial mortgage brokerage shortly after.

While working, I began gaining an interest in sofware engineering, which led me to taking a leap of faith into a highly selective coding bootcamp called App Academy where my entire life was consumed with code for about 80 hours a week for 4 months.
